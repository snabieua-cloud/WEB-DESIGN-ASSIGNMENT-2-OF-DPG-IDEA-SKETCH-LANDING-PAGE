# EduConnect SL
EduConnect SL is a **Digital Public Good (DPG)** platform designed to centralize access to educational opportunities in Sierra Leone. It connects students, teachers, policymakers, and parents to scholarships, digital libraries, and teaching resources.

## 🚀 Features
 **Scholarship Finder** – Discover local and international funding opportunities.  
 **Digital Library** – Access textbooks, research papers, and exam prep materials.  
 **Teacher Resources** – Lesson plans, teaching aids, and collaboration tools.  
 **Policy Dashboards** – Data insights for education officials to design better interventions.  

## 🎯 Target Users
 Secondary school students  
 University students  
 Teachers and educators  
 Policymakers and education officials  
 Parents and guardians
